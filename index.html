The Costpoint Keep-Alive Bookmark

Disclaimer: I won't guarantee that this works well or even that it won't suddenly cause your car's tires to go flat. If you have problems with this bookmark, please file an issue report because it's my fault. Currently I have only tested it on Chrome.

Are you tired of having Costpoint's sessions expire on you? Then behold! I have a bookmark that will check every 10 seconds to see if the "Session Timeout" dialog appeared, and if so, will press the button for you.

To install it click-drag (click, hold, and drag) on the following link and drag it into your Bookmark bar:

Costpoint Keep Alive

To use it:

  • Open and log into Costpoint
  • Open up the Timesheet App in Costpoint
  • Click on your Costpoint Keep Alive bookmark

For the curious, here is the source: 

(function(){
  setInterval(function(){ 
    if (!(window.getComputedStyle(document.getElementById("timeoutDiv")).display === "none")){ 
      document.getElementById("timeoutBtn").click();
    };
  },10000);
  alert("Keep Alive Engaged");
})();
